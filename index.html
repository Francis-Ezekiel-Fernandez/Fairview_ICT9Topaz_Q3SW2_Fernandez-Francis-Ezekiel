Sword Art Online



Synopsis:

Kids mess around with virtual games. Kazuto Kirigaya AKA Kirito beta tests a game called Sword Art Online, when the game is released, he joins the public server and gets stuck as we see him and all others fail to log out. Players get trapped due to the game being made in connection to the way you join, like a headset, the NerveGear is like a helmet that you put on, the game does not allow you to leave and if you die in the game, the NerveGear will send high voltages of electricity into your head killing you. This arc is called the Aincrad Arc, and it is finished when they clear the game, the next seasons focuse on Alfheim Online, Gun Gale Online, and Alicization. These coming seasons show Kirito and how he rises to the top in most of the games and usually is the one to save everybody.
